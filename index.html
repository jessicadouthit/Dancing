Dancing Through Space and Time

- This course will introduce students to the historical, theoretical, and critical elements of dance.

- Students will explore the role that dance has played in society throughout history and in contemporary life.

- Experiences in class will include reading, discussion, video viewing, movement exploration, creative-process activities, and group presentations.
